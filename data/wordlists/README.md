# Russian Word Lists

Generated with `npm run build:ru-wordlists`.

Sources:

- `ru-guesses.json`: five-letter forms from `danakt/russian-words`, MIT license.
- `ru-answers.json`: five-letter words from the intersection of `Harrix/Russian-Nouns`, MIT license, and `Digital-Pushkin-Lab/Russian-Word-Frequency-Lists-for-Children`, CC0-1.0.

Words are normalized to lowercase Russian letters, with `—ë` folded to `–µ`.
